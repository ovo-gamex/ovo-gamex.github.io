OvO Game – Privacy Policy

Learn how OvO GameX collects, protects, and uses your data while you enjoy OvO Game or Ovo Unblocked.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how OvO GameX manages data when you play our game in-browser. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Basic usage data (pages visited, playtime, device type).
  • Anonymous analytics to improve game performance.
  • No personally identifiable information unless voluntarily provided via contact forms.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To optimize the gameplay experience in OvO Game and Ovo Unblocked.
  • To fix bugs, monitor performance, and improve design.
  • To provide player support when you contact us.

4. Cookies and Tracking

OvO GameX may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some game features may be affected.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to safeguard your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics tools or ad networks. These providers may collect non-personal data to serve better game experiences.

7. Your Rights

  • You may request deletion of any personal data you have shared with us.
  • You can opt-out of cookies or analytics tracking.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of OvO Game after updates means you accept the new policy.
